Frequently Asked Questions

1. What shipping options do you offer?

Answer: We offer standard and express shipping options. Free shipping is available for orders above PHP 2,000.

 

2. How much does shipping cost?

Answer: Shipping fees depend on your location and chosen delivery option. You can view the shipping cost at checkout.

 

3. How long will it take to receive my order?

Answer: Delivery times:

  • Metro Manila: 1-2 days for express delivery and 3-5 days for standard shipping.

  • Provincial areas: It will take 4-7 days.
